

Summary of Key Features and Design Strategies in Popular Virtual Pet Apps:
- Customization options
- Mini-games
- In-app purchases
- Various virtual pets to choose from
- Users can care for and nurture their virtual pets

We have browsed through several websites and analyzed virtual pet apps listed on them to identify key features and design strategies used in popular virtual pet apps. As a result, we have found that the most popular virtual pet apps offer customization options, mini-games, in-app purchases, and various virtual pets to choose from. Users can care for and nurture their virtual pets in these apps, which are available on both iOS and Android platforms.
