Virtual Pet Design File

Design Elements:
- Pet appearance (colors, shapes, and sizes)
- Sounds and visuals for relaxing and calming activities
- Interactivity through touch and voice commands

Behaviors:
- Pet movement (e.g., walking, jumping, or flying)
- Pet actions (e.g., eating, sleeping, and playing)
- Pet emotions (e.g., happy, sad, and angry)

Response Characteristics:
- User feedback and pet response
- Virtual breathing techniques to reduce stress and anxiety
