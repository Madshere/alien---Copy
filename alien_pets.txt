

List of Virtual Alien Pets for Our App:
- Chirpee: A small, bird-like creature that is highly energetic and loves to play games. Chirpee is highly social and loves to interact with its owner.
- Squigy: A slimy, amoeba-like creature that is highly adaptable and can change its shape and color to blend in with its environment. Squigy is highly intelligent and can solve complex puzzles.
- Fuzzy: A furry, cuddly creature that is highly affectionate and loves to be petted and held. Fuzzy is highly empathetic and can sense its owner's emotions.
- Spark: A sparkly, glittery creature that is highly excitable and loves to dance and sing. Spark is highly creative and loves to express itself through art and music.
