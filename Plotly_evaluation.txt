Evaluation of Plotly library: 
Plotly is a Python visualization library that provides a variety of plotting options. It is powerful and is well-suited for interactive plots and dashboards, providing a JavaScript-based plot editor, which is compatible with most platforms and browsers. Notable features of Plotly include:
- Ease of use
- Compatibility with a variety of software
- Customization options

Cons:
- Steep learning curve
- Certain custom features require a subscription.
- Occasionally, it may be slow or unresponsive
