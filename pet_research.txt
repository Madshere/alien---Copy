Research on Different Types of Pets:

1. Cats
- Appearance: Soft fur, pointy ears, green eyes
- Behavior: Independent, curious, playful
- Habits: Napping, grooming, hunting

2. Dogs
- Appearance: Shiny coat, floppy ears, wagging tail
- Behavior: Loyal, affectionate, social
- Habits: Walking, playing fetch, playing with toys

3. Birds
- Appearance: Bright plumage, sharp beak, bright eyes
- Behavior: Vocal, social, intelligent
- Habits: Flying, building nests, singing

4. Fish
- Appearance: Colorful scales, fins, gills
- Behavior: Calm, peaceful, low-maintenance
- Habits: Swimming, hiding, playing
